Delete Your Account

You can request permanent deletion of your Befor account and all associated data at any time.

Fastest way: Open the Befor app → Settings → Security → Delete Account. Your account will be deleted immediately.

Request deletion by email

If you no longer have access to the app, send us an email from the address associated with your Befor account.

We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of receiving your request.

Request Account Deletion

Tapping the button opens your email app with a pre-filled deletion request.

⚠️

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. All scan history, credits, and account data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.

What gets deleted

• Your email address and account credentials

• All scan history and results

• Remaining scan credits

• Any saved preferences or settings
